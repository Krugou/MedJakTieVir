Under the Northern Lights

Experience the magic of cinema under the shimmering Northern Lights at the Kirkenes Film Festival. Join us for a mesmerizing journey into the world of film, where storytelling comes to life amidst the breathtaking Arctic landscape.

Discover the Magic of Cinema

Welcome to the Kirkenes Film Festival, where dreams come to life on the silver screen! Our festival is a celebration of storytelling, creativity, and the love for cinema. Join us for a cinematic journey that will transport you to different worlds, make you laugh, cry, and inspire you.

Events During the Festival

  • Opening Night Gala - Join us for a glamorous red carpet event.
  • Screening of Award-Winning Films - Experience cinematic excellence.
  • Q&A Sessions with Filmmakers - Get insights from the creators.
  • Cinema Under the Stars - Enjoy outdoor screenings.
  • Film Workshops and Panels - Learn from industry experts.
